Our Products

Konabi’s products are pre-built, ready-to-deploy solutions designed to jump-start your analytics and automation journey with minimal setup. We might already have exactly what you need, and if not, we’ll build it from the ground up.


Power BI Dashboards

Deliver instant visibility into performance and KPIs with dashboards that turn raw data into actionable insights. Available for systems like Concur T&E, AuditBoard, Oracle HFM, ServiceNow, and more.


Power Automate Flows

Streamline repetitive tasks, reduce errors, and cut costs with automation that integrates directly into your systems. Our readily available Power Automate flows tap into system and dashboard data to send emails and notifications on what matters most to you.


Power Apps

Simplify data collection and process management with apps that are easy to use and tailored to your workflows. Our apps can also integrate with Power BI dashboards, providing a clean user interface for seamless execution of flows.


Copilot Studio Agents

Put AI to work with conversational copilots that guide employees, assist customers, and connect securely to your business data. Our readily available bots are highly customizable and can be deployed to CoPilot Web or Teams.
