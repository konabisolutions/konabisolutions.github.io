Our Services

At Konabi Solutions, we use the Microsoft Power Platform to deliver practical, enterprise-ready solutions that help your business run smarter and faster. We also provide tailored training sessions on Power BI, Power Apps, Power Automate, and Copilot Studio to ensure your team can confidently manage, adapt, and expand solutions long after deployment. Technology delivers the most value when people know how to use it. We are here to help.


Power BI – From Data to Decisions

We design and build dashboards that don’t just show numbers—they tell a story. Our solutions connect to your existing systems, consolidate data, and highlight the metrics that matter. The result is actionable insights that help leaders move from reactive reporting to proactive decision-making.


Power Apps – Streamlined Processes

We create custom apps that replace inefficient spreadsheets, emails, or paper-based processes. Whether it’s managing requests, approvals, or field data collection, our Power Apps reduce manual work, enforce consistency, and make processes easy to use across devices.


Power Automate – Intelligent Automation

Repetitive, manual tasks drain productivity. We implement workflows that automate data entry, document routing, notifications, and approvals, freeing your team to focus on higher-value work. This saves time and reduces costs without disrupting your existing systems.


Copilot Studio – AI Agents for Business

We help you put AI to work where it matters most. With Copilot Studio, we design conversational agents that answer questions, guide employees through processes, or assist customers in real time. These copilots integrate securely with your business data, making AI practical, safe, and useful.
